The Kit Designer game already exists on the Golden Arrows website. Do NOT rebuild it from scratch. Instead, completely redesign and modernize the existing Kit Designer into a premium professional football jersey design studio similar to the attached reference.

Objective

Transform the current cartoonish kit designer into a realistic 3D football jersey customization studio worthy of a professional football club.

The experience should feel similar to designing kits on Nike, Adidas, FIFA, Football Kit Creator or PES/eFootball.

The page must feel premium, interactive and modern.

Overall Goal

The finished page should feel like an official Golden Arrows FC Kit Design Studio, not a casual game. Visitors should feel as though they are designing a real Umbro football jersey for the club. The interface should be polished enough that fans want to spend time creating, saving, and sharing their designs, while maintaining the existing backend functionality and data
