One feature I'd add that most clubs don't have

A "Design of the Week" competition.

Fans create their own Golden Arrows kit or boots, click Submit, and (when you later add a backend) designs are uploaded to the club. Other fans can browse a gallery and vote with one vote per account. Each week, the winning design is featured on the homepage, creating a strong reason for fans to return regularly and engage with the club. This could become one of the platform's standout features.
